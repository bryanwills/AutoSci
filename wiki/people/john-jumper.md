## Research areas

- Protein and biomolecular structure prediction (AlphaFold lead)
- Deep learning for biomolecular modelling and computational biology
- Geometric / invariance-aware neural architectures for molecular systems
- Equivariant attention; diffusion-based denoising for structure

## Recent work

- [[highly-accurate-protein-structure-prediction-alphafold]] — first author and co-corresponding author on AlphaFold2

## My notes

First author and co-corresponding author on AlphaFold2 (Nature 2021); shared the 2024 Nobel Prize in Chemistry for the work. Continued co-authorship on the AlphaFold DB 2024 update reflects the team's commitment to making the predictions usable infrastructure rather than just a research artifact. The continuity of architectural taste from AF2 to AF3 — invariant point attention to diffusion-based denoising — suggests an unusually high tolerance for replacing previously load-bearing components when scale and data permit.
