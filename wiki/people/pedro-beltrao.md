- Protein-protein interactions and structural interactomics

## My notes

Beltrao's group has been a long-running source of phosphoproteomics-functional-impact integration. The 2023 Nat. Struct. Mol. Biol. paper marks the group's pivot toward using deep-learning structure prediction as a routine instrument for systems-level PPI biology.
