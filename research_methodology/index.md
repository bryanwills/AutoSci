# Research Methodology Notebook

User-maintained notes on *how to do research* — process knowledge that is
reusable across projects. This notebook is **not** part of the wiki schema:
it is not an entity type, has no frontmatter contract, and never appears in
the graph, xref rules, or dashboard entity counts. Workflows may *read* these
notes as guidance only when you explicitly opt in, and must never write here.

Boundary:
- `wiki/` — research objects and project memory (papers, concepts, methods,
  topics, manuscripts, reviews).
- `research_methodology/` — your distilled methodology: principles,
  collaboration rules, writing habits, and process checklists.

Do **not** record confidential reviewer text, unpublished experiment details,
specific manuscript versions, or any paper/concept/method facts here — those
belong in `wiki/`, `paper/`, or project artifacts.
